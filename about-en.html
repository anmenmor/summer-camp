About us

At Coconut Camp young people can enjoy a multitude of activities in a wonderful environment surrounded by nature.

We have three centres spread over the northern coast of the Iberian Peninsula: Cantabria, La Coruña and the Basque Country. All of them are very close to the best besaches for surfing, one of the main activities that you can do.

Our qualified and experienced instructors are with young people 24 hours a day. The groups that are formed are maximum 8 peopleper monitor. The team is completed with 2 lifeguards and 2 nurses.
