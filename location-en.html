Location and facilities

Somo (Cantabria).

The main attraction is its kilometer beaches.
It combines excellent conditions for lerning and surfing, with good waves throughout the year.
Our school is located just 7 kilometers from Somo beach. the transfer to it is done by minibus.

Pantín (La Coruña).

Pantin beach is very popular for its natural landscape and because it is held every September thePantín Classic 1, international surfing championship.
Our school is located on the beachfront, being able to access it directly on foot.

Zarautz(País Vasco).

Zarautz beach is the largest in the Basque Country and one of the longest in the Cantabrian.
Our school is located in a natural environment just 10 kilometers from the beach, arriving it by bus.

Accomodation will be in cottages fot up to 8 children, with bathroom insede it and wardrobes. Sheets, blankets and pillows are provided.

Other facilities:

  • Dinning room
  • Workshops rooms
  • Swimmimg pool
  • Nursery
